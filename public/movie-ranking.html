Rudy's Faves

The Five Best Movies of All Time (according to me)

East of Eden

James Dean's absolute best. Monterey, California shines as the backdrop for this gorgeous movie about a father and son.

Dumb and Dumber

Possibly the movie I most quote. It's an instant pick me up when I am having a bad day.

Kingdom of Heaven

My favorite Christopher Nolan movie. Aesthetically it is stunning. The history, the story telling, *chef's kiss*.

Bull Durham

Another one I quote entirely too much. One of those, "if it's on, I am watching" movies.

Dead Poet's Society

"Language was invented for one reason - to woo women." Do I need another reason to love this movie?
